Frequently Asked Questions

How do online therapy sessions work?

All sessions are conducted online from the comfort of your own home. We use a secure video platform, and you can join from anywhere in Ontario. It's just like meeting in person, but more convenient and accessible.

Do you offer a free consultation?

Yes! I offer a free 15-minute consultation so we can chat about what you're going through and see if we're a good fit. There's no pressure‚Äîit's just a conversation to help you decide if therapy feels right for you.

What are your fees and do you accept insurance?

Individual sessions are $150. Sessions can be billed under Social Work or Psychology Benefits with many insurance providers. A sliding scale is available for those who qualify. Please inquire before your appointment about coverage options.

What if I'm not sure therapy is right for me?

That's completely normal! Many people feel uncertain when they first reach out. The free consultation is a great way to explore your concerns without any commitment. We can talk about what you're experiencing and whether therapy might be helpful.

How long does therapy typically last?

It varies! Some people find what they need in a few months, while others benefit from longer-term support. We'll work together to figure out what makes sense for you, and you're always in control of how long you'd like to continue.
